01How does cash on delivery work?
Order online, we call you to confirm, then Yalidine brings your package. Check the piece in front of the courier, pay cash if you're happy. No card, no prepayment, no stress.
02How long does delivery take?
We pack within 24h in Ras El Oued, then Yalidine takes 1–4 days depending on your wilaya. Stop desk pickup is cheaper and usually faster — see the full rate table per wilaya.
03What if the size doesn't fit?
You have 7 days to exchange, and exchange shipping is on us. KOLDWAVE cuts run oversized — check the size guide before ordering for the exact measurements.
04Will sold-out pieces restock?
No. Every drop is a limited run — once a piece sells out, it's gone. Join the newsletter below for early access before the next wave hits.
05Do you really ship to all 58 wilayas?
Almost — Yalidine covers the whole country except a couple of deep-south zones. Home delivery or stop desk, your choice at checkout. Full coverage on the shipping page.
