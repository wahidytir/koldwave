Returns & exchanges

Wrong size or changed your mind? You have 7 days from the day your parcel arrives.

  • The piece must be unworn, unwashed, with tags still attached.
  • DM @koldwave.dz on Instagram or use the Contact page with your order reference.
  • We book a Yalidine pickup, or you drop the parcel at your nearest stop desk.
  • Exchanges ship free. Refunds go out as soon as the piece is back and checked.

Sale items are exchange-only. Beanies and accessories can only be exchanged unopened, for hygiene reasons.
